Conference Overview

EnggCoat 2026 will provide a broader platform for researchers, engineers, and industry to converge, collaborate, and drive advancements in the field of engineering and functional coatings. The conference fosters cross-disciplinary collaboration to address real-world challenges and showcase cutting-edge research.

Conference Topics

• Nickel and chromium plating
• Electro-Galvanizing
• Electroless nickel plating
• Electrodeposited composite coatings
• Anodising and sealing processes
• Hot-dip galvanizing
• Thermal spray coatings
• Thermal barrier coatings
• Physical and chemical vapor deposition
• Plasma Coatings
• Additive manufacturing coatings
• Mechanical plating
• Zn-Al flake coating processes
• Multi-layered nano-crystalline coatings
• Organic coatings and painting
• Smart coatings (anti-corrosion/self-healing)
• Wear and abrasion resistance coatings
• Optical coatings
• Quality control in coating applications
• AI/ML in coating development
• Design of coatings
• Eco-friendly surface treatment processes
• Pretreatment and effluent treatment
• Equipment for coating processes
